How is this weighted?

School Ranking: We use an 85/15 split. The score is 85% based on the feature quality (e.g., Happiness) and 15% based on the number of student reviews.

State Ranking: We calculate a "Weighted Average" of all schools in the state. Larger schools with more reviews have a proportionally higher impact on the state's score.
